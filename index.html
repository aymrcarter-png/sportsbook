How It Works

League Rules

01
Starting Balance
Every player starts with $500 in fake money at the beginning of the season to place bets with.
02
Placing Bets
Log in to the sportsbook, pick a game, choose your side or total, enter your wager and place your bet before kickoff.
03
Payouts
Winnings are calculated using American odds. The commissioner settles all games after they are played.
04
Reward Store
Use your winnings to purchase bragging rights and rewards from the store. Balances carry over from the sportsbook.
05
No Real Money
All currency is fake and for fun only. No real money is involved at any point in The Legacy Sportsbook.
06
Commissioner Calls
All disputes and final decisions are made by the commissioner. Their word is final on all matters.
